# Findings

## Key Findings
- The alert was valid for investigation and should not be ignored.
- The observed traffic required analyst review because it appeared outside normal assumptions.
- The available evidence supported a cautious defensive posture.
- Documentation and escalation logic are essential even when evidence is incomplete.

## Defensive Value
This lab demonstrates that a SOC analyst must:
- investigate before dismissing
- document clearly
- think in terms of risk
- support decisions with evidence

## Final Assessment
The lab reflects a structured analyst mindset built around visibility, verification, and controlled response.
